Welcome to Vishwas Sadan, a well-equipped boys-only PG located just a 5-minute walk from Graphic Era Deemed to be University and 10 minutes from Graphic Era Hill University. With 10 rooms, including 9 two-person rooms and 1 three-person room, we provide a comfortable and focused living environment for serious students. Our facilities include a food mess, 24/7 uninterrupted water supply, and electricity with inverter backup to ensure your stay is hassle-free. We prioritize cleanliness with regular bathroom cleaning every week and offer quick assistance whenever needed. To maintain a safe and study-friendly atmosphere, visitors are only allowed with valid purposes. Vishwas Sadan is your ideal choice for a peaceful and productive student life.
